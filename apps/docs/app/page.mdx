# Introduction to ConvoForm

Welcome to **ConvoForm**, the open-source platform that transforms traditional forms into interactive conversational experiences.

## What is ConvoForm?

ConvoForm leverages AI to turn static forms into engaging conversations. It's designed to be:

- **Conversational**: Users answer questions in a chat-like interface.
- **AI-Powered**: Generates follow-up questions and validates answers intelligently.
- **Easy to Use**: Create forms by simply describing what you need.
